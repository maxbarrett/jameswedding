The Venue

Once the estate of Prime Minister Sir Robert Peel, Hampton Manor was built by his son in 1855 and has now been lovingly restored to offer a stylish setting for the celebrations. The contemporary rooms, stunning gardens and indulgent accommodation make Hampton Manor an unrivalled venue for the big day.
